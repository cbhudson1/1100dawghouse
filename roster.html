1100 Hill House Roster

Jon "The Monument" Broun

Guard / 5'5" / 145

The Monument strikes fear into the opposing teams eyes. Big men dispise playing against the 5'5" beast becuse they know they will be taking elbows to the ribs all game. No one knows why people call him The Monument but the rumor is he made it up himself. His efficient jumpshot will complete any team in The Yard.

Connor "Fundamental" Terech

Center / 6'1" / 205

Terech played center for two years on his highschool varsity team, hence the nickanme Fundamental. While playing the five at 6'1" it was a necessitty that Terech played sound fundamental basketball. Occasionally, Terech shows flashes of his athletic ability he had in highschool. Those sights are far and few between but the fundamentals are always there.

Coop

Guard / 5'10" / 160

Whatever type of weather there is you can expect Coop to be in The Yard. Rumor has it he hoops with gloves on in the snow. Some say he works too hard, he would say he doesn't work hard enough. No one knows if Coop owns any shirt because he never has one on in The Yard.

Jonathan "Bunnies" Jasica

Forward / 6'2" / 180

Unlike The Monument, the nickname Bunnies was earned, not given. He is by far the most athletic player in The Yard. No other washed up athlete comes close to matching Jasica's jumping abilities. Jasica's is a player favorite because it means the rest of the team won't have to work for rebounds.

Freddie "Beanstalk" Baldwin

Forward / 6'4" / 180

Although Baldwin does not enjoy playing in the paint, he spends almost all of his time there because he is stuck guarding the other team's tallest player. If it was up to him, Baldwin would spend his time on the 3-point line, stroking threes with his buttery release. You know Baldwin is getting serious when he walks out with his 90s headband that has not been washed in years.

Logan "Bugman" Roach

Guard / 5'10" / 190

While Roach might appear to have hops in the logo of Backyard Top Shot, the rest of The Yard knows he was three inches off of the ground. Roach's jumper is smooth as a baby's bottom. If there were refs in The Yard, Roach would foul out of every game in the first minute. That doesn't stop him from getting physical in The Yard.
