Welcome to The Dog House!

11 lads. 11 core friends. Welcome to the most iconic house on campus. This is the Dog House. You've seen us, heard us, and probably almost hit us tossing the football. Now, here's more about us.

Encompassing an experience like no other over the course of the last 8 months, this website stands as a testament of the memories and moments we as a house have shared together. Someday we can look back and use these as pages to remember the times we had at 1100 Hill St. and those shared at U-M in general. Enjoy the visit into the 1100 Hill House, #HooksOut.

How to buy a moment?

Owning a play from your favorite players sounds complicated, but Backyard Top Shot makes it easy. There are a couple ways to aquire a moment. The marketplace allows users to buy and sell moments with other users.

The marketplace allows the public to set their own price by judging the demand for certain moments. Another way to get moments is through challenges. Challenges require users to collect a specific amount of moments to aquire exclusive moments only available in challenges. Get started now buying heading to the Marketplace!

What to do with a moment?

Every user has a different motive for collecting moments. Some enjoy building up their collection and collecting as many moment as they can. While others enjoy collecting their favorite players moments and show them off to their friends.

Another reasons why Backyard Top Shot appeals to user is the financial aspect. User attempt to buy moments at a low price and sell them at a higher price. There are many other reasons why users collect moments. Begin collecting now and discover your own unique strategy.
